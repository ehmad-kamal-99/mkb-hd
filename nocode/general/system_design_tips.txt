- System might be something they are working on or have already worked with.
- Do a high-level design first before diving deep into components.
- Start with functional / non-functional requirements.
- Pick 3,4 scenarios that you want to satisfy/achieve.
- Discuss different approaches, and their pros and cons, that can be used for solving those problems.
- Iterate through scenarios one-by-one.
- Pick a database. [sql, nosql, k/v store, graph]
- Draw a sequence diagram. [consider sync vs async approaches]
- Class Diagram of components. [utilize separation of concerns, inheritance, polymorphism]
